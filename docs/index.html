This site is the public publishing surface for Blockchain Jewelry Concept. It hosts collection pages, NFT-ready metadata, and artifacts for series including Spiral Manifold.

About Blockchain Jewelry Concept

Blockchain Jewelry Concept turns Ethereum block activity into sculptural form. By translating block size and sequence into 3D objects, the project treats recorded network time as something that can be collected, displayed, and carried into future AR and VR spaces.

About Spiral Manifold

Spiral Manifold is a collection built from precise 1000-block ranges on Ethereum. Each work corresponds to a specific time span, producing a singular spiral structure whose scale, rhythm, and color come from the blockchain data itself.
